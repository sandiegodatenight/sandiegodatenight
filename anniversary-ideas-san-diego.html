Anniversaries

Anniversary Ideas in
San Diego

By San Diego Date Night Team·Updated April 2025·7 min read

An anniversary deserves more than a nice dinner reservation. San Diego's geography — the bay, the coastline, the desert, the mountains — gives you access to experiences that feel genuinely extraordinary. These are the anniversary ideas that create stories couples tell for decades.

01
Private Yacht Charter on San Diego Bay
A private catamaran for just the two of you — your route, your music, your cocktails, the Coronado Bridge at sunset. The most romantic experience on the bay. Triton Charters offers private charters from $975/hour.
Most Romantic
02
Tasting Menu at Addison
San Diego's only two-Michelin-star restaurant. An extraordinary multi-course tasting menu in an elegant setting at the Grand Del Mar. Reserve months in advance for anniversary weekends.
Fine Dining
03
Hot Air Ballooning Over Temecula
Float over 45+ wineries in the Temecula Valley at sunrise. Most operators include a champagne toast on landing. One of the most genuinely magical experiences within day-trip distance of San Diego.
Adventure
04
Overnight at Hotel del Coronado
California's most iconic beach hotel — the 1888 Victorian landmark on Coronado beach. A night at The Del with dinner at Surf & Sand is a complete and perfect anniversary experience.
Luxury
05
Valle de Guadalupe Wine Day Trip
Baja California's wine valley — often called the Napa of Mexico — is 90 minutes from San Diego. World-class restaurants, organic wineries, and a completely different atmosphere from anything in the US. Passport required.
Day Trip
06
Stargazing Overnight at Anza-Borrego
California's largest state park has some of the darkest skies in Southern California. Book a cabin or glamping tent, bring a star chart, and watch the Milky Way from the desert floor. Extraordinary and genuinely private.
Romantic
Planning an anniversary in San Diego
  • Book private yacht charters and Michelin-starred restaurants at least 4-6 weeks in advance for anniversary weekends.
  • Hotel del Coronado packages often include dinner, spa, and beach access — check their anniversary packages for better value than booking individually.
  • Hot air balloon operators in Temecula typically launch at sunrise — plan to stay overnight in Temecula the night before rather than driving up at 4am.
  • Valle de Guadalupe is best March through October — the harvest season (August-October) is particularly beautiful.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most romantic thing to do for an anniversary in San Diego?
A private yacht charter on San Diego Bay at sunset is consistently the most romantic anniversary experience in the city. For a complete anniversary, combine a private charter with dinner at George's at the Cove or Addison for an unforgettable day.
Where should I stay for an anniversary in San Diego?
Hotel del Coronado is San Diego's most iconic romantic hotel. For a more modern luxury experience, the Pendry Hotel in the Gaslamp Quarter is excellent. La Jolla has several boutique hotels with ocean views that are perfect for anniversary weekends.
How far in advance should I book anniversary experiences in San Diego?
Private yacht charters and Michelin-starred restaurants should be booked 4-8 weeks in advance for weekend anniversaries. Hotel del Coronado books out months in advance for peak season. Hot air balloon operators in Temecula are often available with 2 weeks notice.
